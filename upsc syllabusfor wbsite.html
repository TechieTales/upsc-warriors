Prelims | Mains | Interview – Complete UPSC Syllabus

📝 Prelims – GS Paper 1

  • History of India & Indian National Movement
  • Indian Polity & Governance
  • Economic & Social Development
  • Environmental Ecology, Biodiversity
  • Current Affairs (National & International)

🧠 Prelims – CSAT (Paper 2)

  • Comprehension & Logical Reasoning
  • Basic Numeracy & Data Interpretation
  • Decision Making & Problem Solving
  • Interpersonal Skills & Communication

📚 Mains – GS Paper 1

  • Art & Culture
  • Modern Indian History
  • World History
  • Indian Society
  • Geography – India & World

⚖️ Mains – GS Paper 2

  • Constitution & Polity
  • Governance & Social Justice
  • International Relations

💹 Mains – GS Paper 3

  • Indian Economy
  • Science & Technology
  • Environment
  • Disaster Management
  • Security Issues

🧭 Mains – GS Paper 4

  • Ethics, Integrity & Aptitude
  • Case Studies
  • Emotional Intelligence
  • Public/Civil Services Values

✍️ Essay Paper

  • Two Essays – from diverse topics
  • Focus on structure, balance & examples
  • No syllabus – wide open thinking test

🎙️ Interview (Personality Test)

  • DAF-based Q&A
  • Current Affairs Opinion
  • Communication & Confidence Test
  • Understanding of self, nation & society
